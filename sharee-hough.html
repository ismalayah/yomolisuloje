Early Life of Hough

Hough was born on 22nd July 1977. She is originally from Orem, Utah. Therefore, she holds American citizenship.

Hough was welcomed in a well-to-do family due to which she had a sound childhood. She was brought up by her parents in Utah along with her siblings.
